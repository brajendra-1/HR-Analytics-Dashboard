# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

![Dashboard Preview](dashboard_preview.PNG)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to help organizations understand and reduce employee attrition. This dashboard provides a 360° view of workforce data — from salary bands and education levels to department-wise and age-wise attrition patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze employee attrition across multiple dimensions and help HR teams and business leaders make **data-driven decisions** to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

- ✅ **KPI Cards** — Headcount, Attrition Count, Rate, Avg Age, Salary & Tenure
- ✅ **Department Filter** — Human Resources, Research & Development, Sales
- ✅ **Attrition by Gender** — Male (140) vs Female (79)
- ✅ **Attrition by Education** — Life Sciences, Medical, Marketing, Technical
- ✅ **Attrition by Age Group** — 26-35, 18-25, 36-45, 46-55, 55+
- ✅ **Attrition by Years at Company** — Line chart showing tenure vs exit pattern
- ✅ **Attrition by Salary Slab** — Upto 5K, 5K-10K, 10K-15K, 15K+
- ✅ **Attrition by Department** — Lab Technician, Sales Executive, Research Scientist
- ✅ **Attrition by Job Satisfaction** — Matrix table with satisfaction scores 1–4

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 📉 **16.1% attrition rate** — significantly high, needs immediate HR intervention
- 👨‍🔬 **Laboratory Technicians** have the highest attrition (62 employees)
- 💸 **Employees earning up to ₹5K** are most likely to leave (163 out of 237)
- 🎂 **Age group 26–35** sees the highest attrition (116 employees) — peak career-switching age
- 🎓 **Life Sciences graduates** account for 38% of attrition
- 📅 **Employees who just joined (0 years)** show a sharp attrition spike — onboarding issue
- 👨 **Males (140) leave more** than females (79) in absolute numbers

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop** (free download from Microsoft)
3. Use the **department buttons** at the top to filter by HR, R&D, or Sales
4. All charts and KPIs update **automatically**
